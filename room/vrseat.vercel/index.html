Selecting your 3D seats has never been easier!

This is a product that is under development. It was created to be a middleware between ticket buyers and companies that sell seats in various cinemas, theaters, schools, stadiums, among many other places.

The experience of selecting seats is often boring and does not allow for the correct preview of the experience before purchase. Here, we demonstrate how you can make this experience half immersive using Three.js. It also allows the use of spatial audio, to help the buyer understand how the sound in the room behaves at the selected distance.

If you own a ticketing company and are interested in taking part in the development of this platform, please send us a message. Visit our website for updates.
